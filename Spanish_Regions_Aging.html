GDP per capita of Spanish Regions in the year 2000 and 2019

From the Instituto National de Estadstica and the Instituto Geogrfico Nacional. Euros Inflation Adjusted to 2019


First, I imported data relating to Spains GDP per capita in 2000 and 2019, and from the mapSpain package I imported pobmun19 which is a df including Spains 2019 census. Next, I cleaned up the GDP df in order to merge it with the census df.
